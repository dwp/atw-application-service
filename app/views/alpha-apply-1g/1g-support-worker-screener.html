Does your condition mean that you need a support worker at work?

Access to Work can help to pay for support workers such as job coaches, British Sign Language (BSL) interpreters and note-takers.

Types of support worker

A support worker could be:

  • BSL interpreter
  • carer
  • counsellor
  • driver
  • job aide
  • job coach
  • mentor
  • note-taker
  • personal reader
  • travel buddy
  • other types of support worker
