If you need help applying for Access to Work

You can get help with your application by telephone or email:

  • telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • email: dwponline.helpdesk@dwp.gov.uk

These services are available from Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm and are free to use.

If you cannot hear or speak on the phone

Dial 18001 then 0800 121 7479 to use the Relay UK service.

If you use British Sign Language (BSL)

You can use the video relay service. Watch a video to find out how to use the service.

Accessible information about Access to Work

Information about applying for Access to work is available in these alternative formats:

  • BSL videos with audio and subtitles
  • easy read
  • large print
  • braille
  • audio CD
