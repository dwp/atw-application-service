Based on your answers, you are not eligible for Access to Work

Access to Work gives extra help to make work easier if you have a disability or health condition. If you do not need extra help to make it easier for you to do your work, then you are not eligible for Access to Work.

You may be eligible for one of the following services:

  • Employment and Support Allowance (ESA), if you have a health condition or disability that affects how much you can work
  • Universal Credit, if you have a health condition or disability that prevents you from working or preparing for work
  • Personal Independence Payment (PIP), if you have a long term health condition or disability

You can continue to apply for Access to Work if you still think you're eligible for it.

Contact Access to Work

If you'd like more information about your eligibility for Access to Work, you can contact us by:

  • telephone: 0800 121 7479
