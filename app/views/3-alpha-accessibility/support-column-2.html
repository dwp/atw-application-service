Do you need support to do you job?

Examples of support could be a support worker, special aids and equipment and travel to work assistance.

Get help

Watch the British Sign Language (BSL) video for this question.

You can also get help by:

  • Telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • Textphone: 0800 121 7579
  • Relay UK service, if you cannot hear or speak on the phone: 18001 then 0800 121 7479
  • Video relay service for BSL users (find out how to use the service)
  • British Sign Language (BSL) users can use the video relay service (find out how to use the service)
  • email: dwponline.helpdesk@dwp.gov.uk

These services are available Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

Easy Read guides are also available and you can order other formats such as braille, large print or audio CD.
