Do you need extra help for your disability or health condition to make your work life easier?

Examples of extra help includes support workers, special equipment and help getting to work.

Get help

  • Watch the British Sign Language (BSL) video for this question (opens a new tab).
  • You can also get help by:
  • • telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • • textphone: 0800 121 7579
  • • Relay UK service, if you cannot hear or speak on the phone: 18001 then 0800 121 7479
  • • video relay service for BSL users (find out how to use the service)
  • • email: dwponline.helpdesk@dwp.gov.uk
  • These services are available Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.
  • You can also view the easy read guides or order braille, large print or audio CD formats.
