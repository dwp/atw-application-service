Apply for an Access to Work grant

Check you’re eligible before you apply.

You can apply for Access to Work online or by using the Access to Work helpline.

You’ll need to give:

  • your contact details
  • your workplace address and postcode
  • he name of a workplace contact who can confirm you work there (they will not be contacted without your permission)
  • the email address or work phone number of your workplace contact
  • information about how your condition affects your work and what help you think you need

If you need the form in other formats, such as braille, large print or audio CD, call the Access to Work helpline.

Apply online

Apply with the Access to Work helpline

You can apply by using the Access to Work helpline. You can use the helpline in these ways:

  • Telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • Textphone: 0800 121 7579
  • If you cannot hear or speak on the phone, you can use Relay UK service: 18001 then 0800 121 7479
  • British Sign Language (BSL) users can use the video relay service (find out how to use the service)

These services are available from Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.
