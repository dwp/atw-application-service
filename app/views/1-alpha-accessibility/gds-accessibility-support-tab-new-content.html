Support with your application

You can get support with your application or apply by using the Access to Work helpline. You can use the helpline in these ways:

  • Telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • Textphone: 0800 121 7579
  • If you cannot hear or speak on the phone, you can use Relay UK service: 18001 then 0800 121 7479
  • British Sign Language (BSL) users can use the video relay service (find out how to use the service)

These services are available from Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

More guidance about Access to Work

BSL videos and Easy Read guides are also available for information about Access to Work.

If you need the form in other formats, such as braille, large print or audio CD, call the Access to Work helpline.
