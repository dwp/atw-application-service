Based on your answers, you may be eligible for Access to Work

To apply, you'll need to give:

  • your contact details
  • information about your job and employer
  • your workplace address and postcode
  • the details of a workplace contact, such as a line manager or HR manager, if you're employed by a company or organisation (we will not contact them without your permission)
  • your unique tax reference number (opens in new tab), if you’re self-employed
  • information about how your condition affects your work and what help you think you need

You may be asked to provide quotes for costs, depending on the support you need.
