You may be eligible for Access to Work

To apply, you will need to give:

  • your details
  • information about your job
  • information about how your condition affects your work and what help you think you need
  • if you are employed, the details of someone we can contact at your workplace (we will not contact them without your permission)
  • if you are self-employed, your Unique Taxpayer Reference (opens in new tab)

It is optional to include quotes for some types of support to speed up the processing of your application. If you do give quotes, you only need to give total cost and supplier details.
