Do you need mental health support at work?

This could be things like:

  • one-to-one mental health support from a professional, outside of work
  • a support plan for your mental health
  • practical tools and techniques to help you with your mental health at work

Access to Work mental health support is provided by Able Futures and Remploy (both links open in a new tab). We'll refer your details to one of these providers, but you can also apply to them directly for help.

Your employer will not be contacted about mental health support.
