If you need help applying for Access to Work

You can get help with your application by using the Access to Work helpline or email address. You can contact us in these ways:

  • telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • textphone: 0800 121 7479
  • Relay UK service, if you cannot hear or speak on the phone: 18001 then 0800 121 7479
  • video relay service for British Sign Language (BSL) users (find out how to use this service)
  • email: dwponline.helpdesk@dwp.gov.uk

These services are available from Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

If you don't need support, you can continue to apply online.

Accessible information about Access to Work

We use simple language and clear layouts for our online guidance. You can navigate this using speech recognition software and listen using a screen reader. View the online guidance.

Information about applying for Access to work is available in these alternative formats:

  • BSL videos with audio and subtitles
  • easy read
  • large print
  • braille
  • audio CD
