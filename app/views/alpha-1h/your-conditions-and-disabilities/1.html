What condition or disability do you have?

This can include, for example:

  • a physical disability
  • a mental health condition
  • a hearing or sight impairment
  • a learning disability or difference
  • an illness
  • a temporary condition
  • a developmental condition
  • any other condition, disability or illness not mentioned above

You do not have to be medically diagnosed with a health condition or disability to be eligible.
