You may not be eligible

If you're a civil servant, your employer will provide support instead of Access to Work from April 2022. Contact your employer to arrange this.

If you're not sure if you're a civil servant, speak to your employer.

Access to Work can cover mental health support for civil servants, provided by Able Futures and Remploy (both open in new tabs). You can apply to these suppliers directly for help, or call the Access to Work helpline.

If you think you might still be eligible

If you think you might still be eligible, contact us:

  • telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • email: atwosu.london@dwp.gov.uk

If you cannot hear or speak on the phone

Dial 18001 then 0800 121 7479 to use the Relay UK service

If you use British Sign Language (BSL)

You can use the video relay service. Watch a video to find out how to use the service.

If you need financial support

The government can provide benefits and financial support depending on your situation.
