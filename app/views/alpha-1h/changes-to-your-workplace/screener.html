Does your condition mean that you would benefit from physical changes at your workplace?

Access to Work can help to pay for things like:

  • ramps for wheelchairs to navigate a workplace
  • disabled toilets
  • an audio-visual fire alarm
You should talk to your employer about reasonable adjustments (opens in new tab) before claiming for changes to your workplace using Access to Work.
