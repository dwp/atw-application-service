Do you have a Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR) number?

This is 10 numbers, for example 1234567890. It will be on tax returns and other letters about Corporation Tax. It may be called ‘reference’, ‘UTR’ or ‘official use’. You can find a lost UTR number (opens in a new tab).
